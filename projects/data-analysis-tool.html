FreeBay

A community-driven platform for sustainable living through item sharing and reuse

About the Project

FreeBay is a community-driven web platform that encourages sustainable living by allowing people to give away usable items for free. It's built on the principle of "one person's trash is another's treasure," creating a circular economy that reduces waste and builds stronger communities.

Key Features

  • Post Listings: Easily share clothes, books, furniture, electronics, and more with your local community.
  • Browse Nearby Items: Discover free items available in your locality with an intuitive map interface and search functionality.
  • Connect & Share: Build a culture of reuse, reduce waste, and promote community sharing through direct messaging and item reservations.
  • User Profiles: Build trust through verified profiles and user ratings.
  • Real-time Notifications: Get instant updates when someone is interested in your items or when new items are posted in your area.
